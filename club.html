About the Club

Football for every age and ability in Linslade and the surrounding area

Who We Are

Linslade Galaxy FC is a community football club based at Greenleas Lower School in Linslade, Bedfordshire. We field teams across a wide range of age groups — from U8 all the way through to U17 — for both boys and girls.

Our boys teams compete in the Milton Keynes and District Development League (MKDDL), while our girls teams play in the Bedfordshire FA Girls Football League. Across all sections, we are proud to be an FA Charter Standard club.

We believe football should be accessible, enjoyable, and developmental at every level. Whether your child is just starting out or looking to compete at a higher standard, there is a place for them at Linslade Galaxy.
