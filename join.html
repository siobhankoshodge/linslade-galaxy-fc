Join Linslade Galaxy FC

We welcome new players across all age groups — boys and girls, beginners and experienced

How to Join

Getting started is simple. Here's what to expect.

1

Find Your Team

Browse our teams below by age group. Your child's team is based on their school year, not just their birthday.

2

Get in Touch

Fill in the enquiry form below or email us directly. We'll put you in contact with the relevant team manager.

3

Come for a Trial

Join a training session and meet the team. Trials are informal — just bring boots and enjoy the game.

ℹ️
Age groups explained
Teams are based on school year. U8 = school years 3–4, U9 = year 4, U10 = year 5, U11 = year 6, U12 = year 7, U13 = year 8, U14 = year 9, U15 = year 10, U16 = year 11. If you're unsure which age group applies, just ask us.

What to Expect

Training

Most teams train weekly. Your team manager will share exact times and locations once you're in touch.

Subs & Fees

Club subs cover league registration, pitch hire, and kit. Exact amounts vary by team — ask your team manager for current rates.

Kit

Match kit is provided by the club. Players need their own football boots and shin pads.
